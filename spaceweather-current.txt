<h3><a href="/forecast.html">3-day Solar-Geophysical Forecast</a> issued Oct 13 22:00 UTC </h3>
<p><b>Solar Activity Forecast:</b>       
Solar activity is expected to be low with a chance for M-class flares and a slight chance for X-class flares during the forecast period (14-16 October).  </p>
<p><b>Geophysical Activity Forecast:</b> 
The geomagnetic field is expected to continue at unsettled to active levels with a chance for isolated minor storm periods on day 1 (14 October).  Mostly unsettled conditions are expected on day 2 (15 October) due to persistence.  By day 3 (16 October), quiet to unsettled conditions are expected as coronal hole effects wane.  On days 2-3, there is a slight chance for a greater than 10 MeV proton event at geosynchronous orbit as Region 1589 rotates into a more geoeffective position. </p>
  </blockquote>
